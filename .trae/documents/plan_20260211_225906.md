I will improve the `OriginalPreviewModal` to make it behave more like a simple and functional ebook reader, addressing the issues of the blocked page, lack of trackpad zoom, and difficult movement.

### Changes to `src/components/OriginalPreviewModal.tsx`

1.  **Enable Hand Tool by Default**:
    *   Change the initial state of `isHandToolActive` from `false` to `true`.
    *   This will allow users to immediately "drag" the page to move it, solving the feeling of being "blocked" and "unable to move well".

2.  **Add Native Trackpad Zoom Support (Gestures)**:
    *   Implement event listeners for Safari/macOS native gesture events: `gesturestart`, `gesturechange`, and `gestureend`.
    *   These events are specific to macOS trackpads and are currently missing, which is why "widening the trackpad" (pinch) doesn't work.
    *   I will map the gesture's `scale` property to the component's zoom state, allowing smooth, native-feeling pinch-to-zoom.

### Why this fixes the issues:
*   **"Page blocked / Cannot move well"**: By enabling the Hand Tool by default, the interface will respond to click-and-drag inputs immediately, allowing intuitive panning without needing to find and click a toolbar button.
*   **"Cannot zoom widening the trackpad"**: Adding the missing gesture event listeners explicitly connects the trackpad's pinch motion to the zoom logic.
*   **"Simple and functional"**: These changes remove friction, making the preview feel natural and responsive like a standard ebook reader or PDF viewer.
